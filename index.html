These feeds are a read-only, standards-compliant mirror of Sri Lanka This Week, which is curated & maintained by Nuwan I. Senaratna (@nuuuwan) in the upstream repository nuuuwan/lk_news_digest.

The weekly digest itself is AI-generated (using large language models such as GPT-5) from vetted English-language Sri Lankan news sources, as documented in the upstream project. This site simply exposes stable feed URLs for use with feed readers, apps, and integrations.

Feed implementation & hosting: nandulak/lk-news-weekly-feed. All credit for the original editorial content, data sourcing, and concept belongs to Nuwan I. Senaratna (@nuuuwan). Please refer to the upstream repository for methodology, licensing, and source details.
